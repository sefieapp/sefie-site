Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The app is not working properly.
A: Please restart the app and try again. If the issue continues, contact support.

Q: My photo could not be scored.
A: Make sure your face is clearly visible and try another photo.

Q: Where are my photos stored?
A: Photos are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them.

Q: I made a purchase but did not receive it.
A: Please contact us with your purchase details, and we will assist you.

Response Time
