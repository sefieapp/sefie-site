Privacy Policy for Sefie

Effective Date: February 2026

Sefie ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Sefie mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Photos:
Photos selected by users are processed for analysis purposes only. Photos are securely transmitted to our server for processing to generate results. Photos may be processed using secure processing services necessary for the functionality of the app. All processing is performed under our control and responsibility. Photos are not stored on our servers after processing. Photos are stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly shared by the user.

We do not perform biometric identification or facial recognition.

Device or User Identifier:
The app may use a device-level or app-generated identifier to enable core functionality. This identifier is not used to personally identify users outside the app.

Usage Data:
We may collect limited usage data such as feature usage frequency and app performance information to improve the service.

Purchase Information:
In-app purchases are processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Advertising Data:
We may display third-party advertisements. These services may collect limited data necessary for ad delivery in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not use this data to track users across third-party apps or websites.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Generate photo-based analysis results
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

We do not track users across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Photos are processed only after the user selects them within the app.

3. Data Storage

Photos and analysis results are stored locally on your device.
Photos transmitted for processing are not retained on our servers after processing.
If you delete the app, your locally stored data may be permanently removed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

  • Secure service providers necessary for app functionality (e.g., processing services, analytics, in-app purchase services)
  • Legal obligations if required by law

5. Data Deletion

Users may delete photos and saved data at any time within the app.
Uninstalling the app removes locally stored content.

6. Children’s Privacy

Sefie is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
