Privacy Policy for Sefie

Effective Date: February 2026

Sefie ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Sefie mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Photos:
Photos selected by users are processed for scoring purposes only. Photos are securely transmitted to our server for AI processing to generate scoring results. Photos may be processed using secure third-party AI services. All processing is performed under our control and responsibility. Photos are not stored on our servers after processing. Photos are stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly shared by the user.

We do not perform biometric identification or facial recognition.

Usage Data:
We may collect limited usage data such as feature usage frequency and app performance information to improve the service.

Purchase Information:
In-app purchases are processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Advertising Data:
We may display third-party advertisements. These services may collect limited data necessary for ad delivery. We do not use this data for tracking across third-party apps or websites.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Generate AI-based photo scoring
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

We do not track users across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Users are informed and provide consent before photos are sent for AI processing.

3. Data Storage

Photos and scoring results are stored locally on your device.
Photos transmitted for AI processing are not retained on our servers after processing.
If you delete the app, your locally stored data may be permanently removed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

  • Secure service providers necessary for app functionality (e.g., AI processing services, analytics, in-app purchase services)
  • Legal obligations if required by law

5. Data Deletion

Users may delete photos and saved data at any time within the app.
Uninstalling the app removes locally stored content.

6. Children’s Privacy

Sefie is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
